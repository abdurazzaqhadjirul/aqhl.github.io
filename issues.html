Current Civil Engineering Issues in the Philippines

1. Corruption in projects – misuse of funds and poor-quality work

2. Poor planning/coordination – weak collaboration between agencies

3. Project delays & cost overruns – slow processes and budget issues

4. Climate & disaster risks – typhoons, floods, landslides damage infrastructure

5. Ineffective flood control systems – frequent flooding despite projects

6. Urbanization problems – traffic, drainage, informal settlements

7. Bureaucracy/red tape – slow approvals and complex procedures

8. Funding constraints – inconsistent or delayed infrastructure budgets

9. Lack of skilled workers & modern tech – limited advanced engineering use

10. Sustainability challenges – difficulty implementing eco-friendly designs
